FAQs Registering and getting started What account type can I register as?
There are 3 types of accounts in Finitee: Individual user accounts are appropriate for individuals who wish to use the app to interact with other users and discover businesses and nonprofits and do not have products and services to display. These accounts do not have product sections and have a canvas section for their personal posts. Business accounts are appropriate for businesses that are for-profit and have products and services to display. These accounts have a home section that navigates to multiple product sections that can be used to showcase products and services and interact with visiting users. The business account will only be activated and fully available upon proof of business existence submitted and approved. Nonprofit accounts are appropriate for nonprofits and are similar to business accounts but have an extra donations section that allows the user to setup donation requests that other users can find and respond to. The nonprofit account will only be activated and fully available upon proof of nonprofit status submitted and approved
How do I register an account as an individual user?
Upon starting the app, the login screen will be displayed. Tap on the “Create account” button and a screen will be shown that allows you to select the account type you wish to create. Select “Individual registration” and then tap on the “Choose account type” button. A series of screens will be shown that allow you to enter the information required to register your account. The screen where your phone number is requested will also have links to the legal terms of service and privacy policy and tapping on the Continue button implies your agreement to these conditions. The next screen will require you to enter a one-time password (OTP) sent to the phone number you entered previously. Enter the OTP that you received in your phone SMS to proceed. The next screen in the registration process will request you to enter your email and after that a screen will be shown requesting you to enter a code sent to the email that you entered previously. Navigate to your email and find the email that was sent to you from Finitee and enter the code in the app. The next screen requests that you enter your partial home address information which will be used to provide you with a choice of showing your home location on the map instead of your live location. You only need to enter street level information and are not required to enter unit number or house number information. Your address information will not be shown to any other user. You will next be requested to create a unique username which will be shown to other users and will function as a unique identifier for you. Finally, a “Welcome to Finitee” dialog will be shown to you. Tap on the “Dismiss” button and you will be shown the sonar screen and you can now start using the app.

What is an individual user’s visibility to Businesses and Nonprofits?
Businesses and nonprofits cannot find or view individual users in the sonar or search sections. Businesses and nonprofits will only be able to view the events, sales or services listings of individual users if the user sets the visibility of those listings to allow businesses and nonprofits to view them. Businesses and nonprofits can chat with individual users only if the individual users allow it in their privacy settings. This is because we do not wish to allow businesses and nonprofits to target individual users and subject them to unwanted sales and advertisement campaigns. Individual users can choose to connect with businesses and nonprofits voluntarily and then receive information from them if they so choose.

Navigating the app How do I navigate across the various sections of the app?
At the bottom of every main screen in the app, a navigation menu will be shown with icons of the most commonly used sections of the app that when tapped, navigate the user to those sections. Other sections not covered in the bottom navigation menu, can be navigated to via the rightmost icon on the bottom menu which will open a right menu that will allow the user to navigate to the remaining sections of the app.
What are the Bottom menu icons and what does the More icon (rightmost icon) show?
The bottom menu displays the icons to navigate to the frequently accessed sections of the app, namely: Sonar, Canvas, Inflows, Connections, Search, Chat and the More icon. The More icon opens the remaining sections of the app including: Profile, e-Card, Sales listings, Events, Shopping List, Services, Invite to Finitee, Settings, Help and Logout.
Sonar functionality What is the Sonar section and what can I do there?
The sonar section is a real time GPS view of your location in the world with your location shown as a blue dot icon (or business/nonprofit icon). You can tap on the search icon to open the search tray to enter search criteria and initiate a search for individual users, businesses, nonprofits, events, donation requests, sales and service listings and totems. You can set yourself to be invisible to all other users instantly via the “Make invisible” icon. You can center yourself in the sonar screen via the “Center” icon. Any search results can also be viewed in a list view mode by tapping on the box that shows the number of search results on the top left of the screen. You can change your sonar settings by tapping on the settings icon which will bring up a tray of the settings that can be modified. Individual users can see who is viewing them in the viewing list view screen and who is greeting them in the greeting list view screen.
How do I make a sonar match search?
In the sonar screen, tap on the search icon on the bottom right. A tray with the sonar search criteria will appear. Enter keywords and search filters as you wish and then tap on the “Search” button. The results found that match your search criteria will be shown in the sonar screen via various icons indicating individual users, businesses, nonprofits, totems, events, donation requests and sales and service listings. The number of search results found will be shown on the top left of the screen.
How are searches in the sonar matched for various users?
The searches in the sonar are matched differently for different users/items. Individual users can set up to 5 “trait sections” in their Canvas and businesses and nonprofits can enter 10 trait words in their home screen and 10 trait words in each product section. Keywords entered in the sonar search screen that match the trait words will result in that user/business/nonprofit being shown as an icon on the sonar screen. Totems, events, donation requests and the various listings will be matched by words in their headline and the trait words set in each listing to the keyword(s) entered in your search criteria. Donation requests and events and the listings will also have up to 10 trait words that can be matched for searches.
I have a sonar search result showing as an icon on the sonar. What can I do now?
Any icon that is shown on the sonar as a search result can be tapped on and a caorusel will open at the bottom of the screen with further information and links to that user or item’s screen and other functions.
How do I cancel the search and not show search results anymore?
After a search in the sonar, the search results will be shown as icons on the sonar screen and the number of search results will be shown as a number and a “x” icon at the top left of the sonar screen. Tap on the “x” icon adjacent to the number and the search will be closed and all search results will be removed from the sonar screen.
How can I change my search location?
In the search tray, a location edit box with the current location will be shown. Entering a new valid location in the edit box will change your search location to that entered location and search results will be shown at that location within the specified scope area.
How do I know if someone is viewing me on their Sonar?
Another user can search and find and view you on their sonar screen if your sonar privacy settings allow them. When some other user is viewing you, a viewing icon and number will be shown at the top of the map screen which will inform you the number of other users that are viewing you. Tapping on the “>” icon adjacent to this number, will show a screen where those viewing you will be displayed in a list view and you can decide if you wish to be temporarily invisible to them or not. You can choose to be invisible to them for 60 minutes and this will remove you from their screen and they will no longer be able to search and find and view you for that period of time. You can make yourself invisible to them by tapping on the “On hold” icon adjacent to their name in the viewing list view screen. Business and Nonprofits will not be shown who is viewing them in this manner. Someone is viewing me and I do not want them to view me on their sonar! You can handle this in one of three ways: 1) Bring up the viewing list view screen by tapping on the “>” icon adjacent to the viewing number on the top of the map screen. In the viewing list view screen, locate the user that is viewing you and tap on the ‘On hold” icon next to their name and you will not be shown to that user for 60 minutes. They will also not be shown to you on your sonar screen for 60 minutes as well. 2) Navigate to your settings and your privacy settings and change the “Sonar viewable by” setting to either “Connections only” or “No one”. Keep in mind that doing this will make you invisible to all users who do not fit that category, not just the one user you wish to not be seen by. 3) Tap on the “Make invisible” icon on your sonar screen. You will be immediately made invisible to everyone in the Finitee universe. Keep in mind, though, that no user will be able to find and view you on their sonar when this is set. To make yourself visible again, you can tap on the “Make visible” icon at the same location on your screen and your visibility to other users will be back to your original “Sonar viewable by” setting again.
How do I change my sonar visibility settings?
Open up your sonar settings and change the “Sonar viewable by” setting to any one of the choices of “All Finitee users”, “Connections only” or “No one”. Doing this will make you visible to all users who fit that category and invisible to users who do not fit that category. I want to make myself invisible on the Sonar immediately! Tap on the “Make invisible” icon on your sonar screen. You will not be seen by any Finitee user on their sonar screens. To make yourself visible again, tap on the “Make visible” icon at the same location on the top right of your sonar screen and you will be visible as per your original “Sonar viewable by” setting.
Why am I not seeing any search results on the Sonar after I make a search?
If you are not seeing any search results, then no results exist for your search criteria. It may also be that other users who match your search criteria have their sonar privacy settings set to not let themselves be shown to you. As more and more people join Finitee, you should see more results. In the meantime, try searching with more generic search criteria or make a search in the search section of the app.
What are totems?
Totems are markers on the sonar generated by the Finitee Admin that are open and visible to all users and have their own page where users can post comments and make replies to comments. Totems can be found on the sonar screen and in the search section. Once found, tapping on the totem icon results in the carousel for the totem to be shown to you from where you can then navigate to the main screen of the totem. Totems will be created by Finitee to inform users of places of interest or significant events happening at that area.
Individual user canvas Section What is the canvas section?
In the canvas section, individual users can make and manage posts which can be text only or include pictures/videos. \Users can create trait sections in the canvas section as well in order to showcase their interests. The user’s name, username and profile picture is shown in the canvas section along with the number of connections that they have and the number of other users that are receiving inflows from them. Posts are shown as tiles in chronological order of posting and tapping on a tile opens the post in a new screen where it can be viewed in more detail. A favor icon and beam icon as well as a comments icon is shown below the post. The post can be favored and beamed from this screen. Tapping on the comments icon opens the comments screen for the post where comments can be made and replied to.

Either take a photo using your camera phone or select a valid file from your phone’s files to update your profile picture.
How do I create a post in the canvas section?
In the Canvas screen, tap on the blue “+” icon that is shown on the bottom right of the screen and select either “Post” or “Trait post”. For a Trait post, a dialog will be shown requesting you to enter the name of a new trait section or to select an existing trait section. A create post screen will then be shown where the text of the post can be entered and up to 5 pictures/videos can be added. Tap on the “Create post” button when finished and the post should now be shown on the Canvas screen as the topmost left tile.

Other users cannot make posts in your canvas or product section. They can, however, make comments and replies to comments to posts that you have made
How do I view a post in a Canvas section?
Posts are shown as tiles in chronological order of posting in the Canvas screen. Tap on the tile whose post you wish to view and it will be shown to you in greater detail.
How do I favor a post?
Tap on the tile of the post in the main Canvas screen and the post’s screen will be shown to you. In this screen, a favor icon will be shown below the text and the picture/video of the post that when tapped, will favor the post. You can cancel the favor by tapping on the cancel favor icon that is now shown in place of the favor icon.
What is beaming a post?
Beaming a post is the ability to copy a post from another user’s canvas or product section to your canvas. You can do this if the other user has their privacy setting of “Allow to beam” set to allow other users to beam posts from their account. If your privacy setting of “Allow to beam” allows it, the post can then be beamed again by other users who visit your canvas to their canvas. Each time a post is beamed, it’s beam count is increased by one.
How do I beam a post from someone else’s canvas or product section?
You can beam a post that exists in another user’s canvas or product section by navigating to the screen of the post and tapping on the beam icon which will display the post as a tile in your canvas in chronological order of posting. The beam count for the post will increase by one. If the beam icon is not shown to you, then that user has set their “Allow to beam” setting to not allow you to beam posts from their account and you will not be able to beam their post.
How do I invite a connection to view a post?
You can invite other users to view a post in your canvas. In the post’s screen, an invite to view icon will be shown below the post which, when tapped, will open up a list of your connections. You can choose from the list of connections who you would like to invite to view the post. Tapping the Invite to view button will send a notification of the invitation to view to the selected connection(s). Upon receiving the invitation, the selected connection(s) can choose to navigate to the post and view it and beam it to their own canvas if they so wish.
How do I view the comments of a post?
Navigate to the post’s screen by tapping on the post’s tile in the canvas or product section of a user and tap on the comments icon below the post. The comments screen for the post will be shown where you can view all existing comments and make a comment or reply to a comment.
How do I make a comment in a post?
Navigate to the comments screen of the post and at the bottom of the screen, type the text of your comment in the edit box that says “Write a comment”. To attach a picture or video, tap on the “Attach” icon to bring up a menu where you can select taking a photo from your phone or a video from your phone or select a media file from your phone. Once complete with entering text and media, you can tap on the “Send” icon to publish the comment to the comment screen of the post.
Can I include a picture/video in my comment or reply to a comment?
Yes, you can select one picture or video to be part of your comment including the text of your comment. This is done by tapping on the “Attach” icon to bring up a menu where you can select taking a photo from your phone or a video from your phone or select a media file from your phone.

In your Canvas screen, tap on the “Traits” filter and the trait sections created by you will be shown to you. Similarly, when you are visiting another user’s Canvas, tap on the “Traits” filter and you will be shown their trait sections. In your Canvas screen, tap on the “Beams” filter and all posts beamed from other users by you will be shown to you in chronological order. Similarly, when you are visiting another user’s Canvas, tap on the “Beams” filter and you will be shown their beamed posts. In the Canvas screen when the filter “All” is selected, the beamed posts will be shown in chronological order with non-beamed posts.

Inflows Section What is the Inflows section?
The inflows section is where you receive posts from other users that you have set up to receive posts from without having to actually navigate to their account and view the posts there. To receive inflows from a user, you will have to navigate to their canvas or product screens and set up to receive their inflows there. If a user is your connection, you can navigate to them in the connection tab in your connections section and tap on the “Inflows” icon to start receiving inflows from them. The inflows icon will be shown in color to indicate that you are receiving inflows from that user. Once you are receiving inflows, you can tap on the “inflows” icon to stop receiving inflows from that user. The inflows icon will be shown without color to indicate that you are not receiving inflows from that user You can also delete any and all inflows that you have received in your inflows section at any time. You can also search in the inflows section to find inflows by keyword search.
How do I navigate to my Inflows section?
You can navigate to your inflows by tapping on the “Inflows” icon shown on the navigation menu at the bottom of your phone screen. You will also be navigated to your inflows if you tap on a notification in your notifications informing you that you have a new inflow.
How do I setup to receive Inflows from other users?
To receive inflows from a user, navigate to their canvas or product screen and tap on the 3-dot menu on the top right of the screen and in the menu shown, tap on the “Inflows” choice and the color of the icon in the menu option will change to indicate that you are now receiving inflows from that user. Tap the “Inflows” choice in the menu again and the color of the icon in the menu option will change to indicate that you are not receiving inflows from that user. If a user is your connection, you can navigate to the connection tab in your connections section and tap on the 3-dot menu adjacent to their name and select the “Inflows” menu option to start receiving inflows from them. Tapping the “Inflows” menu choice again will result in not receiving inflows from that user.
How do I find a post in my Inflows?
You can perform a keyword search in your inflows to search and find posts. You can also tap on the filter buttons to be shown only the inflows that match that filter setting.
How do I setup to receive donation requests from a nonprofit in my Inflows?
To receive inflows from a nonprofit’s donation section, navigate to the donation section of the nonprofit by first reaching their home screen and tapping on the 3-dot menu and selecting the “View donation requests” menu option. The Nonprofit’s donation main screen will appear and in the top right of the screen an inflows icon will be shown. Tap on the icon and the color of the icon will change to indicate that you are receiving donation inflows from that nonprofit. Tap the “Inflows” icon again and the color of the icon will change to indicate that you are not receiving donation inflows from that nonprofit.

Why do some users show a chat icon and some don’t?
The chat icon will be shown to you if a user who is not a connection of yours has allowed it in their privacy settings. Any user who is your connection will always have the chat icon shown to you so that you can initiate a chat with them.
How do I refer a connection to another connection?
In the “Connections” tab of the connection section, tap on the 3-dot menu adjacent to a user and select the “Refer user” menu option. What is the difference between removing a connection and blocking a connection?
Removing a connection makes that user no longer your connection but you can still search and find that user in the sonar section and the search section as well as view his account as per his privacy settings. The other user can search and find you based on your privacy settings as well. Blocking a user, results in no longer being connected to that user and not being able to search and find that user as well as the blocked user not being able to search and find you. Neither of you will be able to view each other’s accounts in any way until you unblock the user.
Where do I view users that I have blocked and can I unblock them?
The users that you have blocked will be shown in the “Blocked” tab of your connections section. You will be able to unblock the user by tapping on the “Unblock” button adjacent to their name. Once unblocked, you will be able to search and find them and view their account as per privacy settings. However, “unblocking” a previously blocked user does not make them your connection again and you will have to go through the connection request process again to become their connection.
Search Section What is the Search section and how is it different from Sonar search?
The search section is where you can perform a keyword search and view results in a list fashion with no map location shown. The search location can be set to “Global” which provides results from the entire Finitee universe listed by popularity or “Local” which provides results for your local area listed by popularity. The search results are shown grouped by type of result and within that grouping is shown by popularity of the search result with the more popular user of listing shown higher. In the search section, you can search for users, businesses, nonprofits, totems, events, sales listings, service listings and posts. You can also filter your results by tapping on any of the filter buttons on the top of the search section.
How do I navigate to the search section?
The search section can be navigated to by tapping the “Search” icon in the navigation menu at the bottom of the screen.
Can I search with Boolean phrases like “and” & “or”?
Yes, you can search via the following search operators: • Query (including quotations) • -Query (including quotations) • Query AND Query • Query OR Query • Related: Query

There will be extra input parameters that you can enter keywords for so that you can make more advanced searches. The filter buttons will be available in advanced search as well, in order to perform more specific searches.
What can I do with my search results after I make a search?
You can tap on the search result to be navigated to the related screen for that result. For example, tapping on a sales listing shown as a search result will navigate you to the sales listing screen.
How are search results ranked and shown in Finitee?
In the search section, search results for users, businesses, nonprofits and posts are ranked based on search criteria relevance and popularity. So, results that match the search criteria equally will be ranked based on which search result is more popular based on a number of criteria. This provides a fair and balanced ranking system that is more relevant and helpful to the user making the search. Search results for sales listings, service listings and totems are ranked on search criteria relevance and distance from the user making the search.
Why am I not seeing any search results after I make a search?
This is because no user or item in the Finitee universe matches your search criteria. Some users and listings may have visibility settings set to not allow you to view them. Try simplifying your search criteria. As more people join Finitee, you will see more results.
Chat Section What is the chat section?
The chat section is where users can send and receive direct messages with each other. Chat messages can have pictures attached to them. Currently, only one-on-one chat threads are supported.
How do I start a chat?
Navigate to the chat section via the “Chat” icon shown on the navigation menu at the bottom of the screen. In the chat screen, tap on the “+” icon and a dialog with a list of your connections will be shown. You can select the connection with whom you would like to chat and the chat screen for the thread will be shown where you can type text in the edit box at the bottom of the screen and attach a picture to the message and send the message by tapping the “Send” arrow icon.
Can I chat with a user who is not my connection?
Yes, you can chat with a user who is not your connection if they allow it in their privacy settings. You cannot start a new chat thread with a user who is not your connection from the chat section but you can start a chat thread from the canvas, home screen, sales listing, service listing or donation listing screen of other users who are not your connections. You will be able to do this via the chat icon that is shown in those screens if their privacy settings allow you to chat with them. Also, if your privacy setting of “Receive messages from users that are not connections” is enabled, then other users who are not your connections will be able to initiate chat messages with you in a similar manner.
How do I archive a chat thread?
Navigate to your chat section and long press on a chat thread to select it. Upon selection, menu options will be shown on the top of the screen including a 3-dot menu. Tap on the 3-dot menu and select the “Archive” menu option. The thread will be archived and can be found in the archived sub section.
How do I un-archive a chat thread?
Navigate to your chat section. If you have archived threads, at the bottom of the main screen, a link will be shown that is labelled “You have x archived threads”. Tap on this link and you will be shown your archived threads in chronological order by date/time of archiving. Select the thread you wish to un-archive by long pressing on it and then tap on the 3-dot menu that appears on the top right of the screen and select the “Un-archive” option. The thread will be removed from the archive section and will be shown in the main chat screen along with other existing threads in chronological order of the last chat message that was sent within it.
How do I navigate to a user’s canvas or home screen from the chat?
In the chat screen the other user’s profile picture and name will be shown on the top of the screen. Tap on the name or profile picture of the user and you will be navigated to the canvas/home screen of that user. If the user has set his privacy settings to not allow you to view his canvas, then you will be shown his summary screen. If a user does not allow you to view both his Canvas and summary screen then that user will not be shown to you as a search result.
How do I mute a chat thread that I have?
In the chat main screen, long press on the thread that you wish to mute and menu choices will be shown on the top of the screen. Tap on the mute icon and the chat thread will be muted and you will not be notified of any new messages received within that thread.
How do I delete a chat thread?
In the chat main screen, long press on the thread that you wish to delete and menu choices will be shown on the top of the screen. Tap on the delete icon shown on the top of the screen and a confirmation dialog will be shown requesting confirmation that you really wish to delete the thread. Confirm in the confirmation dialog and the thread will be deleted from your chat section. Keep in mind that the thread will still exist in the other user’s chat section.
How do I delete an individual message within a chat thread?
Navigate to the chat message screen within the thread and long press on the message that you wish to delete and menu choices will be shown on the top of the screen. Tap on the delete icon shown on the top of the screen and a confirmation dialog will be shown requesting confirmation that you really wish to delete the message. Confirm in the confirmation dialog and the message will be deleted from the thread in your chat section. If the message was sent by you to the other user, then it will not be deleted in the thread of the other user if he has already viewed it. If the other user hasn’t viewed the message yet, the message will be deleted in his thread as well. A message sent by the other user will not be deleted from his thread when you delete it in your thread.
How do I copy, forward or reply to a message?
Navigate to the chat message screen for the thread and long press on the message that you wish to copy, forward or reply to and menu choices will be shown on the top of the screen. Select either the copy, forward or reply icon in the menu to perform the chosen action.
How do I create and send a broadcast message?

A dialog will be shown that allows you to choose which type of chat thread you wish to start. Select the “Broadcast” option and a new screen will be shown with your connections listed. Select the connection(s) that you wish to send the broadcast message to and then tap on the “Broadcast” button. You will be shown the message screen for the broadcast where you can enter text and attach picture/video and send the message by tapping the “Send” icon.
Can I report a user in the chat section?
Yes. Navigate to the main chat screen and long press the thread that you have with the user and the menu options will appear on the top of the screen. Tap on the 3-dot menu on the top right and then select “Report”. A dialog will be shown to you where you can report the user to the Finitee admin.
Can I block a user in the chat section?
Yes. Navigate to the main chat screen and long press the thread that you have with the user and the menu options will appear on the top of the screen. Tap on the 3-dot menu on the top right and then select “Block”. A confirmation dialog will be shown requesting confirmation that you really wish to block the selected user. Confirm in the confirmation dialog and you and the selected user will be blocked from each other.
Sales Listings What is a sales listing?
A sales listing is a screen that you can create in your sales listings section to display an item that you wish to display to other users in order to potentially sell it to them. The sales listing screen includes the title of the item, the price you wish to get for it, the description of the item, the condition of the item, trait words for the listing, the visibility of the listing, the location and location offset choice for the listing and up to 5 pictures/videos. There is a small fee that every user has to pay to list the sales listing. Once listed, the listing can be found by other users (as per the visibility of the listing) via search keywords that match words in the title of the listing. Searches can be performed in both the sonar section and search section and tapping on a search result navigates a user to the full listing screen for the item. A visiting user can then tap on the “Direct message” button to initiate a chat with the selling user to get more information and communicate further. No actual transaction occurs within the Finitee app. Buying and selling users are matched to each other using the sales listing functionality and any transaction regarding the item will be conducted by them outside the app.
How do I create a sales listing?
Navigate to the sales listing section by tapping on the sales listing choice in the main menu. The sales listing main screen will be shown and any current sales listings will be displayed in chronological order of listing creation. Tap on the “+” icon and a new screen will be shown where you can enter the sales listing fields for a new sales listing. Enter valid information and tap on the “Create” button. You will be shown a dialog informing you that once payment is made and the listing is active, you will not be able to make any changes to the listing except for the price. Tap the “Continue” button to proceed or “Cancel” to go back to the create listing screen. You will next be shown a payment screen to pay for the sales listing. Select the payment type and make the payment after which you will be shown a dialog informing you that the payment was successful and the listing is active. The listing will be active for 30 days after which it will no longer be found and shown to searching users.
Can I edit a sales listing once it is active?
You can only modify the price of a sales listing once it is active. To change the price, navigate to the main sales listing screen and tap on the edit button adjacent to the listing. The screen to edit the listing will be shown with only the price field being editable and all other fields shown as read-only. Edit the price of the listing and tap on the “Save” button. The listing will be active with the new price and will be shown to other users with the new price.
Can a sales listing be searched and found on the Sonar?

Can a sales listing be searched and found in the search section?
Yes, a sales listing can be searched for in the search section via keywords and more specifically the “Sales listings” filter. Search keywords that match words in the sales listing’s title and/or trait words will result in the listing being found and displayed as a search result in the search section.

In the sonar screen, tap on the sales listing icon that was found and a carousel at the bottom of the screen will be shown to you. Tap on the sales listing title or picture in the carousel and you will be navigated to the sales listing page for the item where you can view further information for the item. You can tap on the “Direct message” button to navigate to a chat message thread with the seller of the item and message further with the seller. If the sales listing was found in the search section, simply tap on the search result to be navigated to the sales listing page for the item and then tap on the “Direct message” button as described above.

No, the app only matches buyers and sellers to each other. Any transaction including exchange of money or goods must be performed outside the app.
My sales listing expired and nobody bought the item. Can I renew the sales listing?
Yes, you can renew the sales listing for the same fee for another 30 days. Navigate to the main sales listings page and tap on the expired sales listing. The listing page for the sales listing will be shown. To renew, tap on the “Renew” button and the payment screen will appear as before when you originally posted the listing. Make the payment and the sales listing will be active for another 30 days and will be shown to searching users as per visibility settings of the sales listing. If you do not wish to renew the sales listing, you can delete the sales listing or simply do nothing and the expired listing will automatically disappear from the expired section after 7 days.

Individual user eCard and Rolodex What is an eCard?
An eCard is an electronic version of the paper business card that we are all familiar with. Finitee has the eCard section in the app that allows you to create and manage your eCard and manage its visibility settings. If you allow it, other users will be able to “get” your eCard and “refer” your eCard to their connections. You will also be able to “get” other user’s eCards and store them in your Rolodex if they allow it in their settings.
How do I setup an eCard?
Navigate to your eCard section via the main menu. Your eCard will be shown pre-filled with the values your entered in your profile section. The profile picture will be the same as what was setup in the profile section. You will be able to set the visibility settings for each field in your eCard.
Why can I only change the visibility settings on my eCard fields in the eCard section?
The fields in the eCard are the same as in the profile section of individual users. Therefore, editing the values of the fields is allowed only in the profile screen to ensure integrity of the field values. Only the visibility settings can be changed in the eCard screen. To change the values of the fields, please navigate to the profile screen and make changes there.
How do I change the information in my eCard?
Navigate to your profile screen and make changes to the values there. Once you have made the changes in that section, the values of the edited fields will be shown updated in the eCard section.
How do I “get” another user’s eCard?
Navigate to the other user’s eCard via the 3-dot menu on their canvas or the “eCard” link on their summary screen. Tap on the “Get eCard” button on the top right of the eCard screen and the user’s eCard will be added to your Rolodex. You can also scan the QR code shown on the eCard/eProfile screen of another user’s phone. To do this, navigate to your Rolodex and tap on the “Scan” button at the bottom of the screen and direct the scanner shown on your phone to the QR code shown on the other user’s phone and the other user’s eCard/eProfile will be added to your rolodex.
Can some other user “get” my eCard?

Business home screen and product screens What is the business home screen and what are product sections?
The home screen for a business or nonprofit is the main screen that a visiting user will be shown when he navigates to the account of a business or nonprofit. The Home screen displays the Name of the business/nonprofit, the logo picture, the connect icon, the connected members button, the chat icon and the inflows and endorsements information. What is the “Adjust location on sonar” function for businesses/nonprofits?
A business or nonprofit may wish to adjust their location on the sonar as the position on the sonar extracted via their address entered may be slightly inaccurate. In the home screen, tap on the edit icon and in the edit screen that opens, tap on the “Adjust location on sonar” link and the sonar screen will be shown with the current location of the business/nonprofit displayed on the sonar. Adjust the position of the business using your finger and then when it is in the correct location, tap on the “Save to this location” button. What charges does Finitee apply to businesses?
Businesses are charged a small fee when a visiting user navigates to any of the business’ product section(s). The visiting user is allowed to leave the product section and come back for a period of time and not be charged again. But after a longer interval if the same user again visits a business’ product section, then the fee will be applied to the business again. Also, if a visiting user views a post that includes a video, then a small fee will be charged to the business by the app. Once a user has viewed a video, he will be able to view it again without the business incurring further charges,The fees are accumulated and billed to the business at the end of the month. Nonprofits do not have these viewing charges applied to them.
Why are businesses charged for product section views and video views?
Finitee does not show ads to users. Rather, it has a pay per view or “hits” model for businesses that are displaying their products and services where a visiting user voluntarily navigates to their account rather than an ad being “forced” on them and the business pays Finitee a small fee for the functionality provided. As it is a pay per view based model, if no users visit the product section of a business, then the business will not pay Finitee anything. If there are a large number of users visiting a business’ product section(s) then there will be a correspondingly larger amount payable to Finitee but the business will have that many users viewing and interacting in their product section which should be attractive to the business as this could result in more sales. The idea is to offer businesses a cost-effective model of marketing and branding their products and services rather than expensive and ineffective ads that are mostly disliked by users. Businesses will utilize this model best if they post interesting and engaging content to attract more users to their account and interact efficiently with the visiting users using the posting and direct chat messaging functionality.
How does a business make a payment to Finitee?
The business user will be notified by email and notifications that he has a balance due with Finitee on the 1st of every month for the previous month. To pay, navigate to the “Settings” section via the hamburger menu and under the “Payments” sub-section, tap on the “Make one-time payment” menu option. A new screen will be shown that displays the current balance owed and the payment options. Select the payment option of your choice and make the payment and you will be paid up till the next month. Please note that failure to make the payment will result in loss of functionality to your account and the eventual termination of your account.

Once you have a code, apply it by navigating to the “Settings” section via the hamburger menu and under the “Payments” sub-section, tapping on the “Enter discount code” menu option. The discounted rate will be applied to your business from this point till the end of the discount period for all product section views and video views made by visiting users.

Terminate Account How do I terminate my account and what happens to my data?
To terminate your account, navigate to “Settings” via the hamburger menu and under it, tap on the “Account management” option. The “Account management” screen will be shown. Tap on the “Terminate account” button in the bottom of the screen and the Terminate account screen will be shown. Enter your login password and check the “I understand I will lose all my account data” checkbox and tap on the “Terminate my account” button. Your account will be terminated and an email will be sent to your email address informing you of the termination of your account. All data including posts, pictures, videos, comments etc. will no longer be available to you anymore. If you log in within 30 days, your account will be restored and all data including posts, pictures, videos, comments etc. will be shown as before. However, after 30 days, you will not be able to login to the account any more.
I have terminated my account. Now can I use the email and phone number to create a new account?
No, you cannot use the email and phone number of the terminated account to create a new account. If you wish to create a new account with Finitee, you will need to enter a different phone number and email address during the registration of the new account.
